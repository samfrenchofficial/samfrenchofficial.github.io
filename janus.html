Janus

Brand Identity & Visual Design / 2025

The Vision

The Janus project explores duality in design. We aimed to create a visual system that feels both ancient and futuristic, balancing sharp typography with organic forms.

Execution

By utilizing a high-contrast color palette and bold, structural layouts, the Janus identity commands attention while maintaining a sense of professional minimalism.
